About Us

One stop center for School Tuition from 1st-12th class of CBSE/ICSE/STATE Board

Genius Coaching Classes has been surging ahead on the success track and has distinguished itself by setting up a new standard in the world of competitive education since 2006. It is an Institution of knowledge under the aegis of Mrs. Flavia Lancy D’Souza for imparting the quality education for the students of IX & X, both State Board Syllabus and CBSE Board Syllabus. Our qualified and experienced teachers conduct coaching classes, at convenient timings. Genius Coaching Classes is structured on the principle of consistency and hardwork and believes in scientific approach based on perfect planning and proper guidance. Student’s overall development and splendid success is our prime concern. Genius teaches all subjects under one roof. The limited strength of students in each class, its unique working schedule, pre-planning of complete syllabus, and its strict implementation, personal attention to every student, perfect working pattern, sincere and devout teachers, regular Unit-Tests, Board Pattern-wise preparation of every question paper, etc. has produced altogether a number of merit holders in X Karnataka State Board Exams and in CBSE Board Exams at Genius Coaching Classes.

Why Us?

We at genious aim to develop competitive attitude amongst our students with equal focus on social responsibility, ethics and personal development. It is very important to cater the required skills for a student because today’s young minds will be the leader of tomorrow. But without proper guidance this sounds like building a castle in the air. The institute provides assistance to its students in both English and Hindi medium for better understanding. We provide regular tuitions, revision classes, home tuitions, small group for tuitions so that our educationists can focus on all the children. We also take personal feedback of the children in reference with the knowledge imparted to them at regular intervals. We at genious believe in professional teaching within a homely environment. Therefore to serve this need, we have a team of over 10 teachers from various fields of study. Each of them has sufficient experience in their respective field. We are very supportive in employing women as a part of our team. Providing them a platform to showcase their talent and opening new door towards various career opportunities. Also, relieving them of their financial constraints.
